Deck OLED vs ROG Ally Z1E

Rog Ally's advantages compared to Deck OLED:
  • Z1E often delivers a 5-10% performance boost (can be less powerful in some cases).
  • Windows compatibility with many software applications like editing software and Microsoft exclusives.
  • 1080p, 120hz screen adjustable to 720p.
  • Speakers have slightly more bass.
Steamdeck OLED advantages compared to ROG Ally Z1E:
  • Runs Native SteamOS, Trackpads, User friendly.
  • Better battery, Emulation king, OLED screen, Plugins, and more.
Disadvantages of ROG Ally Z1E:
  • Windows issues, blue screens, constant updates.
  • SD card slot burns, driver issues, poor battery life, etc.
Disadvantages of Steamdeck OLED:
  • Less compatible with Windows-only applications.
  • Lower screen resolution compared to Ally.
  • Static on wired headphones, etc.
